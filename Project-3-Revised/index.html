Audio Cues and Interactions

Playback Threshold Cues

As the song progresses, visual cues will appear at specific points:

  • START - Cue appears at the start of the song (0%).
  • QUARTER - Cue appears when 25% of the song is completed.
  • HALF - Cue appears when 50% of the song is completed.
  • THREE_QUARTERS - Cue appears when 75% of the song is completed.
  • END - Cue appears at the end of the song (100%).

Lyric Sync Cues

Lyrics will sync in real-time with playback, highlighting the current line based on time codes embedded within the lyrics. The current line will be bolded and auto-scrolled into view as the song plays.

User Interaction Cues

The following cues and behaviors respond to user interactions:

  • Scrubbing/Seeking: Dragging the progress slider allows you to jump to different parts of the song. The playback position updates in real-time, and threshold flags reset after scrubbing.
  • Song Change: Selecting a different song will reset playback and load new lyrics and time codes for the selected song.
